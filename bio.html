Flexibility is our business

How many bands do you know that can start the evening with light jazz during cocktails, play a set of blues during dinner and finish the evening with three sets of dance hits from the '70s to now?

Pleased to meet you, too.

We are a fun-loving, talented mix of Calgary musicians and we have been filling dance floors since 2002. We have kept the beat alive through blown breakers and hurricanes — once even on the same night in Cuba. That was exciting.
